FAQ

  • General
  • Washing

  • general

  • What is waterless washing and how does it work?

    Waterless washing is the leading edge of environmentally friendly car washing methods. This method utilizes a high lubricity premixed spray detailer to saturate a panel and then it is wiped off carefully, removing dirt and grime, with microfiber towels.

  • How does mocowash help you save time?

    At mocowash we understand time is a limited resource which is why we bring our services to you while you work. No more hour long detours to your local car wash! We’ll have your car done before you leave work! Please check with us when we will be at your location next!

  • How much water will I save by using mocowash?

    Machine tunnel washes and hose and bucket washes can waste between 40-100 gallons of water per car. By utilizing waterless wash methods, we only use 5-8 oz. of water per car. That’s less than a half a bottle of water used!

  • Washing

  • Will Waterless Car Wash scratch my car’s paint?

    No. Our biodegradable waterless products contain surfactants and polymers to help lift and encapsulate dirt away from the car’s paint.

  • What if I my car is extremely dirty (mud, salt or sand)?

    In extreme conditions, we recommend running the car through a power wash at the local self-serve coin wash first.

  • Do I need to wax my car after getting a mocowash?

    Our waterless product contains carnauba wax so that every time we wash your car, a layer of protection is being left behind to protect your car’s paint.

  • What does mocowash mean?

    The “moco” in mocowash is short for mobile eco.
